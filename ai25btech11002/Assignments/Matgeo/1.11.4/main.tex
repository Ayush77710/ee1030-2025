		\textbf{Question}:\newline

		A vector of magnitude 9 units in the direction of the vector $-2\hat{\imath}-\hat{\jmath}+2\hat{k}$

		\textbf{Solution:}
		The vector with magnitude 9 in the same direction would be: 
		\begin{align}
			\|c\myvec{-2 & -1 & 2}^T\| = 9
		\end{align}
		\begin{align}
		\therefore |c|=3
		\end{align}
		Thus, the vector is:
		\begin{align}
			\myvec{-6 \\ -3\\6}
		\end{align}
